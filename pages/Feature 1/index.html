History

Yujiro Hanma

Also known as The Ogre, is the main antagonist of Baki the Grappler series, as well as the father of the eponymous protagonist. Yujiro is the most dangerous and strongest character in the story, capable of many inhuman physical acts.

Emi Akezawa

Emi Akezawa (朱沢 江珠, Akezawa Emi) is a fictional character from anime and manga series Baki the Grappler. She was the lover of Yuujirou Hanma and the mother of Baki Hanma. Emi was the wealthy leader of the Akezawa Group, and former wife of Eiichi Akezawa.

Jack Hanma is the major recurring character of the martial arts anime and manga series, Baki the Grappler. He is the first son of Yuujiro Hanma, the legendary Strongest Creature on Earth and the half-older brother of Baki Hanma.

Tokogawa

Mitsunari Tokugawa (徳川 光成, Tokugawa Mitsunari) is a fictional character from anime and manga series of Baki the Grappler. Tokugawa is an old man of very small stature who runs the Underground Arena beneath the Tokyo Dome, where fighters of any style compete in hand-to-hand combat with no rules.

General Strydum and Yujiro Hanma

A captain and later general in the US Army and Yuujirou Hanma's companion and comrade-at-arms. He developed an admiration for Yuujirou, and now he travels, fighting with him on the battlefields across the world. He somewhat understands Yuujirou's mentality and mentions that he is the only person to bring the United States to its knees. However, it is often seen that Strydum cannot stand situations in which Yuujirou abuses his brutality and ruthlessness. He seems to know him well and understand his wild way of thinking, but he usually disagrees with his actions. Strydum is also a friend and mentor of Baki's, who keeps him up to date about Yuujirou's whereabouts.

Kozue Matsumoto

Kozue Matsumoto is a fictional character from anime and manga series of Baki the Grappler. She is Baki Hanma's schoolmate and the daughter of Kinuyo Matsumoto, Baki's landlady. In later parts of the series, Kozue is also his girlfriend. Her father fought and died in the Underground Arena.
